Premium Savings Account - Product Terms and Conditions

Effective Date: 1 January 2026

1. Account Overview
The Premium Savings Account is a variable-rate savings account designed for individual customers aged 18 and over. This account offers competitive interest rates on balances maintained above the minimum threshold.

2. Interest Rate
Annual interest rate: 4.5% AER (Annual Equivalent Rate). Interest is calculated daily on the cleared balance and paid monthly into the account on the last business day of each calendar month. The rate is variable and may change with 30 days written notice.

3. Deposit Requirements
Minimum deposit to open: £500. The minimum balance to earn interest is £500. There is no maximum limit on individual deposits, however the maximum account balance permitted is £1,000,000 (one million pounds).

4. Withdrawals
Up to three penalty-free withdrawals per calendar month. Additional withdrawals incur a fee of £10 per transaction. Withdrawals may be made via online banking, telephone banking, or in-branch. Minimum withdrawal amount: £1. Funds are typically available within one business day.

5. Account Management
Statements are provided monthly via online banking. Paper statements are available on request for a fee of £2 per statement. Account holders may appoint one additional signatory to the account.
